HEESEUNG


  • Born:October 15, 2001 (Age 24 years)
  • Full Name: Lee Heeseung
  • Height: 1.82 m
  • Nationality: South Korean

Lee Heeseung is a south korean singer under HYBE Label. He best known as the main vocalist and the oldest member of Enhypen.
On june 1,2020 Heeseung was introduced as a contestant in the CJ ENM and Big Hit entertainment's reality show I-Land,on september 18,2020 he ranked 5'th place by global votes and became a member of Enhypen.

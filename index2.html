overview

ENHYPEN is a south korean boy band formed by Beliftlab. The group consists of seven multinational members JUNGWON, HEESEUNG, JAY, JAKE, SUNGHOON, SUNOO, and NIKI. They were the finalists of a global music competition show I-LAND.

The group officially debuted on November 30th, 2020 with the song "given and tiken".

Enhypen are known for their dynamic and highly synchronized performances, which garnered them international praise as the "4'th generation hot icons" and "Global K-POP Rising Stars."
They are fstest k-pop boy group to become a million seller and the first and only k-pop act in history to have all of their albums RIAJ certified.

Their fandom name is Engene which is derived from the words "engine" and "gene". ENGENE are the engines that help them keep going and the genes that share them the same DNA as the group to connect, discover, and grow together.

Name

The name Enhypen was introduced during the live broadcast of the final episode of I-LAND, the group derives their name from the hypen symbol (-), representing "connection,discovery & growth.
similar to how a hypen connect different words to create new meanings, Enhypen aims "to come together to connect, discover and grow together to form a new act".
